Key Combinations - Item Manipulation (Cont.)

Examine(E) AND Any Mouse Click: Examine

Grab(Ctrl) AND Mouse1 (On Held Weapon): Wield with Right Hand
Grab(Ctrl) AND Mouse2 (On Held Weapon): Wield with Left Hand

Quick Self(Z) AND Mouse1: Activate Right Object
Quick Self(Z) AND Mouse2: Activate Left Object

Alternative Action(Alt) AND Mouse1: Right Object Alternative Action
Alternative Action(Alt) AND Mouse2: Left Object Alternative Action

Double Tap Block(C) AND Movement: Dodge in that direction (3 second cooldown).
Hold Block(C) AND Any Mouse Click, Empty Hand: Dodge towards the target (3 second cooldown).
Hold Block(C) within 1 second of attack: Parry incoming damage (3 second cooldown).
