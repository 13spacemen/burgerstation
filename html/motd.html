Welcome to Nar-sie's Realm. Please be sure to read the rules below.

Well this isn't really a rule. Just a suggestion. Rule 0: Listen to the Admins

If an admin tells you to stop doing something, stop doing it no matter how wrong they are; even if you're absolutely in the right. If you believe that an admin is giving you unfair treatment, talk to Burger. If the admin in question is Burger, then call them a fucking asshole.

Rule 1: Don't be a dick

We're all here to have fun. Please to not harass, bait, or attempt to anger players for the sake of angering players. This includes in character and out of character actions.

We will never ban for using game mechanics, however will will talk to players who use them to provoke other players needlessly. For example, if you target the same player over and over again just for the sake of targeting the same player over and over again, we'll kindly ask you to chill and target someone else. General rule of thumb; if you do something that makes the game reasonably unenjoyable for others, you're breaking this rule.

Racial slurs, racism, hard bigotry, and general bullshit is not tolerated here. Sure, call other people liggers, niggas, and faggots when appropriate, but don't go overboard by dropping hard Rs or FBI crime statistics. In the case that someone doesn't like you calling them insults that aren't specifically against the rules, then be an adult and don't call them those things. Continuing to insult someone after they asked you nicely to stop will be breaking rule 1. We don't care if you think that faggot isn't that offensive, if someone asks you to stop calling them a faggot then you should listen.

Spamming and generally being an eye or earsore is considering being a dick. While the server has an anti-spam feature, it is made by me, so there are probably ways to get through it.

Spoiling quests, the story, or secrets in general is also a big no-no. Regardless of how big the spoiler is, do not give out story points or secrets in OOC, full stop. Go ahead and private message someone who asks, but don't drop secrets in OOC, on discord, or the forums.

Avoid excessive salting. You will die in this game, that's a given. You might lose an item that took you a while to get, but so have others before you and so will others after you. It is generally a very bad idea to aggressively call out a person who killed you because you will very likely be laughed at. If you think someone is being a dick to you on purpose, talk to an admin. If you have a tendency to get salty from being killed perhaps you should find IC means of protecting your equipment from being looted...

Rule 2: Don't cheat

In the rare instance where it is possible for you to exploit the fuck out of the shitcode in place, don't do it. I mean sure I'll forgive you if you exploit the bug once and then report it, but if I find out that you somehow managed to duplicate 1000 admin-only weapons and handed them out to 60 different players, then I'm going to be very disappointed.

IC in OOC doesn't exist here. Talking about things that happen IC in OOC is fine unless it violates the other rules.

Metagaming is fine. Powergaming is fine. Use both as long as you're not a dick about it.

Rule 3: Keep it real-ish

Don't set your character name to something stupid like "asdfghjkl;'" or something incredibly racist. Set it to something like "Bob" or "Bob Beans" or "Bob the Pussy Slayer". Be creative; but don't be annoying.

Keep it cool with the OOC in IC. This rarely happens, but try to avoid excessively mentioning ooc stuff in character.

You don't have to roleplay, but don't ruin other people's roleplay while in safezones. I mean this is also considered under the "Don't be a dick" rule but don't go up to two people flirting with eachother and emoting shitting on the floor despite how fucking hilarious that is.

Do not post NSFW links or sites. Full stop.

Rule 5: Understand the risks

Admins will never compensate you for lost items unless the cause of the lost items was a result of another player breaking rules. Admins will not give back lost items due to lag or other misadventures, even if it's caused by the server. If you have a weak internet connection, consider discovering a way in game to keep all your items even after death. Note that in the very rare case of a server crash, your progress since your last save will be lost. You won't lose all your items upon a server crash.

Save data can get corrupted sometimes. If your save data was corrupted, talk to Burger CALMLY AND POLITELY and they'll fix it.

If in the case where you're absolutely wrecking the server and somehow defeating every single karma-related thing thrown at you and murdering every single player, admins might intervene with meme power at escalating levels. Perhaps we'll put a special reward on your head for killing you... perhaps we'll summon several robots... perhaps we'll summon Nar-sie himself.
